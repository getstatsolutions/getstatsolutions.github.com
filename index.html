Welcome To GetStat Solutions

Yu-Ping Li, Ph.D. founded GetStat Solutions, LLC in 2015 and is the Principal Statistical Consultant. She is a statistician with over 23 years of hands-on experience in design and analysis of clinical trials and medical research in both the pharmaceutical/biotech industry - having worked at Theravance Biopharma, Scios (Johnson & Johnson), Abgenix (acquired by Amgen), diaDexus, and Covance - and academia within UCSF and VA Medical Center. Recently, Yu-Ping was the principal statistical consultant overseeing biometrics support for Afferent Pharmaceuticals (acquired by Merck & Co), formed a partnership with Stage Gate Partners, and currently cooperate with Menlo Therapeutics and Loxo Oncology.
